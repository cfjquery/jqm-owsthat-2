Owsthat

This is the old game of Owsthat.It is played with two, six sided bar dice and a paper scorecard. One die, the batting die, is labeled 1,2,3,4, 'owzthat' and 6. The second die, umpire die, is labeled 'bowled', 'stumped', 'caught', 'not out', 'no ball', and 'L.B.W.'.

Choose two teams and then in the main game window.

TAP the BAT BUTTON to hit the ball.

OR swipe left to hit the ball.
